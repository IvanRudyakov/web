About Us

Cyclomatic Extension is our little corner of the internet where math and physics meet humor. We're a couple of lifelong learners who believe that education shines brightest when it's delivered with a smile. Our goal is to show how much fun problem solving can be when you let your personality shine through every calculation.

With each video we produce, we aim to teach real concepts while sprinkling in jokes, goofy skits, and the occasional dramatic reenactment of a physics problem. It's all about making complex ideas accessible and giving you a reason to laugh along the way.
